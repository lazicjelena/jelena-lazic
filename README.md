## 🔬 Research Focus

My research explores **emotional speech modeling** using **surprisal theory and machine learning**, combining:

* Transformer-based language models (GPT-2, BERT, BERTić, YugoGPT, RoBERTa, ALBERT)
* Signal processing and prosody analysis
* Information-theoretic approaches to speech

I work with real-world datasets (10k+ utterances, 20+ hours of speech) and develop models that explain **temporal variation in speech**.
